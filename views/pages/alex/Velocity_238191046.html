Alex Cook : Velocity

Created by Alex Cook on Mar 03, 2022

Indicates the average amount of Product Backlog that can be turned into a “Done” Increment during a Sprint

It does NOT do the following

  • Show performance
  • Promise Delivery in the future
  • Accurate comparison of Scrum Teams or Individuals
  • Show “how hard” a team is working
