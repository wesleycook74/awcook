The result of a Sprint Review should be an adapted Product Backlog that better follows the feedback of stakeholders, the market trends, actual value data and trends.

***A sprint review can include a demo but a demo is not a replacement for the sprint review***


Tips

  • Be explicit about what you are requesting feedback on
  • Make feedback sessions interactive, encourage participation
  • Enable inter-stakeholder collaboration
  • Make collaboration visible
  • Break-out sessions
