Alex Cook : Daily Scrum/Standup

Created by Alex Cook on Mar 03, 2022

A 15 minute meeting to build the plan for the next 24 hours

  • Promotes self-organization
  • Creates focus and reduces waste
  • Promotes transparency and a shared understanding of progress of valuable outcomes


At the end every team member should understand progress toward the sprint goal, the current plan, the likelihood of achieving it and the plans for the next 24 hours
