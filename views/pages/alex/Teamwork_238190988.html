Alex Cook : Teamwork

Created by Alex Cook on Mar 03, 2022

Feeds empiricism by promoting:

  • Cross-functional
    • If the team has a good understanding of the skills the team has it is easier to work together to create an increment
  • Self-organizing
    • By understanding each other the team can organize itself to provide the best work and value
  • Collaborative
    • Break down silos
  • Stable

Too much shift between teams breaks down the ability of a team to self-organize, collaborate, and will not be able to reach peak performance
